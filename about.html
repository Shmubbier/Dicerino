History/About Us

We are Mara and Sal, an LGBT couple of seven years, and the creators behind Dicerino. Our journey began by accident in December of 2022 when a friend invited us to sell at an event. For fun, we decided to try it out. At the time, Mara, my partner, had been experimenting with resin art for a while, and since we had leftover molds from her previous projects, we decided to use them. Initially, we started by selling keychains, but from the very beginning, we knew that if we were to continue, we would focus on making dice.

Both of us are passionate about Dungeons & Dragons, and as avid players, we realized that dice are an essential part of the game. It seemed like a fun and rewarding challenge to create custom dice, and frankly, you can never have enough dice. As we moved forward, Mara took the time to research what was required to produce high-quality dice. She discovered that making dice was a completely different process from regular resin art. This realization meant that if we wanted to pursue this business, we would need to fully commit, especially since the equipment and dice molds were quite expensive.
